Our Story

About Pizzeria Napoli

Founded in 2010, Pizzeria Napoli was born from a deep love for authentic Italian cuisine. Our founder traveled across Italy, learning traditional recipes from master pizzaiolos in Naples, Rome, and Florence. Today, we bring those authentic flavors to your table with every pizza we craft.

We believe that great pizza starts with great ingredients. That's why we source our tomatoes from San Marzano, our mozzarella from local dairies, and our flour from artisan mills. Every pizza is hand-stretched and baked to perfection in our 900°F wood-fired oven.
